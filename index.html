About Perpetual Trading Academy

Our Mission

To educate and train individuals on stocks, commodities, futures, options, and trading strategies based on different styles. We provide simplified and pragmatic financial education focused on creating financially independent individuals.

Our Approach

We believe in practical, hands-on learning that translates directly to real-world trading scenarios. Established in 2009, our academy has helped hundreds of students achieve financial independence through our structured programs.

Why Choose Us?

  • 20+ years of trading experience
  • Practical, real-world focused training
  • Partnered with Sharekhan for trading accounts
  • Both online and offline learning options

Meet Our Founder

Mr. Aurel David

Stock Broker & Trading Instructor

With over 20 years of experience in the financial markets, Mr. Aurel David is a seasoned expert in stocks, commodities, futures, options, and mutual funds. Operating from Bangalore, he has dedicated his career to simplifying complex financial concepts and making them accessible to traders of all levels.

His pragmatic approach to trading education focuses on real-world application rather than theoretical knowledge. This hands-on methodology has helped hundreds of students achieve financial independence through trading.

Mr. David's partnership with Sharekhan ensures that students have access to reliable trading platforms and resources to implement what they learn in the academy.
